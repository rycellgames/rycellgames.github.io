1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information directly. However, we use third-party services that collect technical data, including:

a. Umami Analytics

We use Umami to help understand how users use our website. Umami collects:

  • IP address (used to estimate approximate location)

  • Browser type, version, and language

  • Pages visited, time spent, and referring sites

  • Cookies to track returning visitors anonymously

b. Cloudflare Web Analytics

Cloudflare collects non-personally identifiable technical data such as:

  • Page views

  • Browser/device types

  • Referring URLs
    Cloudflare does not use cookies or track individual users.

c. Google AdSense

We use AdSense to serve ads. Google may use:

  • Cookies and tracking technologies

  • User data (e.g., device info, IP, browsing behavior)
    This may include personalized ads based on your activity. You can manage your preferences or opt out at Google Ads Settings.

While we aim to work with trusted services, we don’t control how these third parties handle or secure your data. By using the site, you understand that any data shared with third-party tools is subject to their own policies and security practices, not ours.

Our site is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe this has occurred, contact us.
