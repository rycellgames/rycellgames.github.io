10 Minutes Till Dawn Unblocked

10 Minutes Till Dawn is a fast-paced, top-down roguelike shooter where you survive waves of monsters for 10 minutes using unique weapons, characters, and upgrade builds. It combines bullet hell mechanics with strategic skill selection, growing more chaotic and challenging as time goes on!

How to play:

  • Move: W A S D

  • Aim: Mouse

  • Shoot: Left-click (hold to auto-fire)

  • Reload: R

  • Level up: Gain XP to choose from random upgrades
    Survive the full 10 minutes without dying to win, have fun!
