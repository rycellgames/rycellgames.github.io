1 Unblocked

1 Unblocked is a puzzle game based on the original 2640 game, where you have to merge blocks with their numbers to get to the number 2640. This puzzle game is amazing to play in the middle of class, the office, or home. It can improve your logistic skills with daily practice, testing your brain to see if you can find the best way to merge the blocks.

How to play

Your goal is to get from the 2640 block to the 1 block, to do this, you:

  • Use your arrow keys to move all the blocks in the direction of your choice.
  • When the blocks with the same number, they merge into a smaller number. (Half of that number)
  • Every move you make, another block spawns, make sure you don’t run out of space!
